Common Diseases and Solutions

Common Cold

Rest and stay hydrated. Over-the-counter medications like decongestants and antihistamines can relieve symptoms. Gargle salt water for a sore throat. Consult a doctor if symptoms persist beyond 10 days.

Seasonal Allergies (Hay Fever)

Seasonal allergies are caused by allergens such as pollen, leading to symptoms like sneezing, itchy eyes, and nasal congestion. Solutions: Avoid exposure to allergens when possible (e.g., stay indoors on high pollen days). Use antihistamines or nasal sprays to alleviate symptoms. Consider immunotherapy (allergy shots) for long-term relief. Keep windows closed and use air purifiers.

Gastroenteritis (Stomach Flu)

Stay hydrated with water, broths, or electrolyte solutions. Avoid solid foods until vomiting subsides; then gradually reintroduce bland foods like rice or bananas. Practice good hygiene to prevent spreading the virus. Seek medical attention if dehydration occurs or symptoms are severe.
